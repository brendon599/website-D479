Arriving and Exploring Around Taniti!

Getting Here!

  • Most visitors arrive by air

  • Small airport currently supports small jets and propeller planes

  • Airport expansion underway for larger aircraft

  • Small cruise ship visits once per week in Yellow Leaf Bay

Getting Around!

  • Public Buses in Taniti City (5am - 11pm)

  • Private buses across the island

  • Taxis are available in Taniti City

  • Rental cars are available near airport

  • Bikes rentals with helmets required by law

  • Merriton Landing is easy to explore on foot
