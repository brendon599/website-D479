Activities

    beach days, snorkeling, chartered fishing tours, and boat excursions

  • Adventure Experiences:

    rainforest hikes, zip-lining, volcano visits, and helicopter rides

    scenic boat and bus tours around the island

    pubs, microbreweries, and dance clubs

    movie theaters, arcades, bowling alleys and visiting art galleries
