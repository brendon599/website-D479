Welcome to taniti!

Taniti is a small tropical Pacific island under 500 square miles with diverse terrain, including beaches, rainforests, mountains, and an active volcano. It has a population of about 20,000, and its economy was traditionally based on fishing and agriculture before tourism increased.


Things to do

  • Visis wonderful beaches, volcano and rainforest.

  • Experience a variety of cuisines.

  • The Merriton Landing entertainment and nightlife.

Quick info

  • US dollar, euros and yen are accepted.

  • English widely spoken by younger residents

  • Visitors arrive by air or cruise
