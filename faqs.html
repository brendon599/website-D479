Frequently Asked Questions

  • What type of power outlets does Taniti use?

    Taniti uses 120-volt outlets like the U.S.

  • What currency is used in Taniti?

    The official currency is the U.S. dollar. However, many businesses also accept euros and yen.

  • Can I use credit cards in Taniti?

    Yes, many businesses accept major credit cards and banks are available for currency exchange services.

  • Is English spoken in Taniti?

    Yes, especially by younger people. Rural areas may have limited English.

  • What are alcohol rules in Taniti?

    Alcohol not served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

  • What is the drinking age?

    18, but enforcement is relaxed.

  • Is Taniti safe?

    Yes,violent crime is rare. Theft can occur in some areas.

  • What medical care is available?

    One hospital and several clinics with multilingual staff.

  • Are places open on holidays?

    Many attractions and restaurants may close on national holidays.
